Introduction to GIS

Syllabus »

This site contains information describing the course expectations, logistics, and weekly readings available through Google Drive. The getting started sections explain how to (1) set up your Google Drive shared folder where you will place your weekly labs and homework, (2) how to extract and organize the data necessary for your weekly homework, and (3) explain how to contribute maps to our weekly slide critiques. When in doubt check the syllabus or email me directly.
