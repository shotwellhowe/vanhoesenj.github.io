Information

This page serves as a resource to crowdsource constraints on the spatial extent of glacial lakes in Vermont. Our hope is that the geologic community working on projects related to Lake Vermont and the Champlain Sea will offer their insight into the extent of each lake level.

We encourage users to explore the three maps below and add points to the map that willh help us create an improved map of Glacial Lake Vermont and the Champlain Sea.
