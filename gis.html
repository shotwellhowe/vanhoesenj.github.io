Syllabus »

This site contains information describing the course expectations, logistics, and weekly readings available through Google Drive. The getting started sections explain (1) how to set up your Google Drive shared folder where you will place your weekly labs and homework, (2) how to extract and organize the data necessary for your weekly homework, (3) how to contribute maps to our weekly slide critiques and (4) elaborate on the goals and expectations of your final project. When in doubt check the syllabus or email me directly.

Google Drive

All your work for this class will be uploaded to a shared Google Drive folder rather than using Moodle dropboxes. This should address any upload issues you have experienced using Moodle and makes it easier for me to comment on your shared documents. Please follow these instructions to create and share your Google Drive folder with me.


Homework should be uploaded to your shared folder by midnight on the day it is due and weekly labs should be uploaded prior to the start of lab on Wednesday (1:00PM). See the syllabus regarding my no late work policy.

Obtaining Homework Data

You have weekly readings and homework assignments from the Master ArcGIS textbook. These primarily serve as a means to give you mileage with the software and break down the common fear that you can break the software. You need to spend time exploring, clicking and right-clicking within the interface to develop a comfortable level where you can stop thinking about the interface and focus more on the analyses. These exercises typically lead you through the questions and operations step-by-step, however you will likely encounter some issues. Don't hesitate to reach out with questions but if you find yourself spending more than 10-15 minutes on a single question, move on to the next question (if you can); the point of these exercises is mileage not to encourage frustration.


Please review these guidelines as a reminder for how I would like your homework assignments named and shared.

Contributing Weekly Maps

In the weeks before you start working on your service-learning projects, I want you to add a map (static or interactive) to a shared Google presentation that we will explore each Tuesday. So add your maps prior to the start of class each Tuesday.


Please go here each week to add any maps you find to the Google slide deck. If you find an interactive map you would like to share, please take a screenshot and include a link to the online map.

Service Learning Project

During the last 5-6 weeks of the semester your will select a community-partner to work with on a problem or project that integrates GIS to explore or create and issue or create a map to inform their stakeholders. Please read these guidelines, which elborate on the format and expectations for these projects. You will be working with professionals in a variety of fields and disciplines and I have high expectations for the quality of your interactions and final products.
