Google Drive

All your work for this class will be uploaded to a shared Google Drive folder rather than using Moodle dropboxes. This should address any upload issues you have experienced using Moodle and makes it easier for me to comment on your shared documents. Please follow these instructions to create and share your Google Drive folder with me.

Final Portfolio

Students will create a personal portfolio assessing their overall learning, their strengths and weaknesses, and their continuing goals. This portfolio will be compiled electronically and shared through an electronic medium best suited to each student’s goals. Portfolio materials will include, but not necessarily be limited to, electronic samples of student’s best work. Each of the portfolio sections are outlined below and include sections covering:   Personal assessment and goals essay Extracurricular and personal skills assessment Resume and Cover Letter Examples of ‘best’ work Faculty References and Letter of Recommendations Gap assessment Evaluate online presence (defined loosely and likely LinkedIn) .
